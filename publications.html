Recent Publications

Reforming the United Nations Security Council: Perspectives from Emerging Powers

Smith, J., Chen, L., & Kumar, R.

Global Governance Review, Vol. 28, No. 1 (2026) | DOI: 10.1234/ggr.2026.001

This article examines proposals for Security Council reform from the perspective of emerging powers, analyzing their demands for greater representation and the challenges of achieving consensus on institutional change.

Climate Finance and the Paris Agreement: Bridging the Implementation Gap

Johnson, M., & Patel, A.

Working Paper Series No. 45 (January 2026)

This working paper investigates the challenges in mobilizing climate finance for developing countries and proposes innovative mechanisms to enhance the implementation of Paris Agreement commitments.

Digital Sovereignty and Global Data Governance: Competing Visions

Garcia, E., Liu, Y., & O'Brien, K.

Policy Brief No. 12 (December 2025)

An analysis of emerging approaches to data governance, examining tensions between digital sovereignty, cross-border data flows, and the development of international regulatory frameworks.

The Role of Regional Organizations in Conflict Prevention: Lessons from Africa and Asia

Okafor, N., & Tanaka, H.

Journal of International Relations, Vol. 52, No. 4 (2025) | DOI: 10.1234/jir.2025.052

This comparative study analyzes the conflict prevention mechanisms of regional organizations in Africa and Asia, identifying best practices and challenges in regional security governance.

Global Health Governance after COVID-19: Building Resilient Systems

Williams, S., Ahmed, F., & Silva, R.

Special Report (November 2025)

A comprehensive examination of lessons learned from the COVID-19 pandemic and recommendations for strengthening global health governance institutions and preparedness mechanisms.
