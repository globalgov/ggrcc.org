About GGRCC

The Global Governance Research Centre Consortium (GGRCC) is a collaborative network of research institutions and scholars dedicated to advancing the study of global governance, international relations, and transnational policy-making.

Our Mission

Our mission is to foster rigorous, innovative research that addresses the most pressing challenges in global governance. We bring together diverse perspectives and methodologies to contribute to both academic scholarship and practical policy solutions.

Founding Members

The GGRCC was established by six leading research institutions committed to advancing the study of global governance:

Global Governance Centre

Geneva Graduate Institute, Switzerland

CIRGOM

Centre interdisciplinaire de recherche sur la gouvernance mondiale, Université Laval, Canada

SCGG

Stockholm Centre for Global Governance, Sweden

Robert Schuman Centre

European University Institute (EUI), Italy

Niehaus Center

Niehaus Center for Globalization and Governance, Princeton University, USA

ICGG

Institute on Global Conflict and Cooperation, University of California San Diego, USA

What We Do

Research

We conduct interdisciplinary research on global governance institutions, international law, climate policy, development, and more.

Collaboration

We facilitate partnerships between researchers, institutions, and practitioners across the globe.

Capacity Building

We support emerging scholars and early-career researchers through mentorship and training programs.

Policy Engagement

We engage with policymakers and international organizations to translate research into actionable insights.

Knowledge Dissemination

We share our findings through publications, conferences, and public engagement activities.

Networking

We provide a platform for researchers to connect, share ideas, and develop collaborative projects.

Our Values

GGRCC is guided by principles of academic excellence, intellectual integrity, inclusivity, and a commitment to addressing global challenges through evidence-based research. We believe in the power of collaboration and dialogue to advance understanding and promote positive change.

Join Us

We welcome researchers, institutions, and organizations interested in contributing to the study of global governance. Whether you're looking to participate in our events, collaborate on research projects, or simply stay informed about our work, we encourage you to get involved.
